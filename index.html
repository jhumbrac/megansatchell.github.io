About

vie flanerie is a style blog dedicated to the curation and exploration of women's fashion and world travel.

It highlights women's fashion, street style, culture and travel through digestible style vignettes.

vie flanerie loosely translates to "life explored" in French, and takes its inspiration from the spirit of 19th century Parisian flaneurs and their curiosity towards street style, and city culture.
